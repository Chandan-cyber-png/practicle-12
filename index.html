Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computer science education, research, and innovation, preparing graduates who contribute to the global technological landscape and drive sustainable societal growth.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality education in computer science and engineering to develop strong fundamentals.
  • Foster innovation, creativity and problem-solving skills through project-based learning and research.
  • Collaborate with industry and research institutions for practical exposure and internships.
  • Instill ethical values, leadership qualities, and social responsibility among students.
  • Continuously update curriculum and pedagogy to match technological advances and industry needs.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

PSO1

Ability to apply computational thinking and mathematical foundations to analyze complex engineering problems.

PSO2

Design, develop, and implement efficient software and systems that meet user and societal needs using modern tools and techniques.

PSO3

Engage in lifelong learning, research, and professional development to adapt to evolving technologies.
